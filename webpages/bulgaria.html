Discover Bulgaria

A Land of Mountains, Monasteries, and the Black Sea

About Bulgaria

Bulgaria is located in Southeast Europe, bordered by the Black Sea to the east. With a history dating back to ancient Thracian times, Bulgaria is one of Europe's oldest countries and has a rich cultural and historical heritage.

The capital, Sofia, is one of the oldest cities in Europe, blending Roman, Ottoman, and Soviet architecture and influences.

Did you know? The Cyrillic alphabet originated in Bulgaria in the 9th century!

Geography

Bulgaria offers a mix of mountains, plains, and seaside landscapes:

  • The Balkan and Rhodope mountain ranges offer great hiking and skiing
  • The Danube River forms the northern border with Romania
  • The Black Sea coast features resorts like Varna and Burgas
  • Rich biodiversity in national parks such as Rila and Pirin

Culture

Bulgaria's culture is shaped by centuries of Eastern Orthodox tradition, folk heritage, and classical influences:

  • The Bulgarian Orthodox Church and ancient monasteries
  • Colorful folk festivals with music, dance, and costumes
  • Traditional food like banitsa, shopska salad, and yogurt
  • The Cyrillic alphabet is an essential part of national identity
