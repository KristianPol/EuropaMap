Welcome to Bulgaria

About Bulgaria

Bulgaria, officially known as the Republic of Bulgaria, is a country in Southeast Europe known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning natural landscapes. The capital city, Sofia, is a vibrant hub of history, culture, and modern life, while the country is also famous for its ancient Thracian heritage, medieval monasteries, and Black Sea coastline.

Geography

Bulgaria is bordered by Romania to the north, Serbia and North Macedonia to the west, Greece and Turkey to the south, and the Black Sea to the east. The country is characterized by its diverse geography, including the Balkan Mountains, fertile plains, and the picturesque Black Sea coast. Bulgaria is also home to numerous rivers, lakes, and natural parks.

Culture

Bulgaria has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its Thracian, Slavic, and Byzantine roots. The country is known for its traditional music, dance, and festivals, as well as its Orthodox Christian heritage. Bulgarian cuisine is a highlight, with dishes like Banitsa (cheese pastry), Kebapche (grilled meat), and Tarator (cold soup) being popular staples.

Tourism

Bulgaria is a popular tourist destination, offering a mix of natural beauty, historic landmarks, and cultural experiences. Popular attractions include the Rila Monastery, the ancient city of Plovdiv, the Seven Rila Lakes, and the Black Sea resorts like Sunny Beach and Nessebar. Visitors can enjoy activities such as hiking, skiing, exploring medieval sites, and relaxing on the beach.
