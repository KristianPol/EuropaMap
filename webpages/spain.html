Discover Spain

About Spain

Spain, located on the Iberian Peninsula, is a country known for its vibrant culture, rich history, diverse landscapes, and passionate people. From the lively streets of Madrid to the beaches of Costa del Sol, Spain offers endless experiences.

Did you know? Spain has the third-largest number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in the world!

Geography

Spain’s geography is incredibly varied:

  • The Pyrenees Mountains in the north
  • The sun-drenched beaches of the Mediterranean coast
  • The green hills of Galicia
  • The Canary and Balearic Islands

Culture

Spanish culture is lively and passionate:

  • Flamenco music and dance
  • World-famous cuisine such as paella, tapas, and churros
  • Festivals like La Tomatina and Running of the Bulls
  • Architectural wonders by Antoni Gaudí, including the Sagrada Família

Tourism Highlights

Must-see attractions in Spain include:

  • The Alhambra in Granada
  • Park Güell and Sagrada Família in Barcelona
  • The Prado Museum in Madrid
  • Seville’s Alcázar Palace and Cathedral
  • La Rambla and Gothic Quarter in Barcelona
