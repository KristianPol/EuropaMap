Welcome to Spain

About Spain

Spain is a country in Southwestern Europe known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes. The capital city, Madrid, is famous for its vibrant arts scene and historic landmarks.

Geography

Spain features a variety of landscapes, from the sunny beaches of Costa del Sol to the rugged mountains of the Pyrenees and the rolling plains of Andalusia.

Culture

Spain has a rich cultural heritage, famous for its Flamenco music and dance, delicious cuisine like paella and tapas, and its famous festivals such as La Tomatina and Running of the Bulls.

Tourism

Spain is one of the world's top travel destinations, offering iconic attractions such as the Alhambra in Granada, the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, and the Prado Museum in Madrid.
