About Estonia

Estonia is a Northern European country known for its stunning natural landscapes, advanced digital society, and rich cultural heritage. The capital city, Tallinn, is famous for its well-preserved medieval Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Geography

Estonia is characterized by its diverse geography, including over 1,500 islands, dense forests, and numerous lakes. It is bordered by the Baltic Sea, the Gulf of Finland, Latvia, and Russia.

Culture

Estonia has a unique cultural identity, with a strong tradition of choral singing, folk dance, and literature. The country is also known for its Song and Dance Festival, which is one of the largest amateur choral events in the world.
