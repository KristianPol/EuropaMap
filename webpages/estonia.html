Discover Estonia

A Baltic Gem of Forests, Castles, and Digital Wonders

About Estonia

Estonia, located in Northern Europe, is one of the three Baltic states bordered by the Baltic Sea, Russia, and Latvia. It's renowned for its medieval towns, tech-forward society, and pristine natural beauty.

The capital, Tallinn, is known for its well-preserved Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and for being a hub of digital innovation.

Did you know? Estonia is the first country to offer e-Residency—a digital identity available to global citizens!

Geography

Estonia features a varied landscape of forests, lakes, and over 2,000 islands:

  • Bordered by the Baltic Sea with a rugged coastline
  • Saaremaa and Hiiumaa are its largest islands
  • Over 50% of the country is covered by forests
  • Home to bogs, lakes, and diverse wildlife

Culture

Estonian culture blends ancient traditions with modern technology:

  • Famous for its massive song festivals and choral tradition
  • One of the most digitally advanced societies in the world
  • Medieval architecture and folklore still thrive
  • Rich in seasonal traditions and nature-based lifestyles

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Estonia include:

Tallinn Old Town

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with cobblestone streets and Gothic architecture.

Lahemaa National Park

Estonia’s largest national park with forests, bogs, and 18th-century manor houses.

Saaremaa Island

A serene island known for its windmills, spas, and ancient fortresses.
