Discover Estonia

About Estonia

Estonia, located in Northern Europe on the Baltic Sea, is known for its enchanting medieval towns, unspoiled nature, and advanced digital society. With a history influenced by both East and West, Estonia today offers a unique blend of tradition and innovation. The capital, Tallinn, boasts one of the best-preserved medieval Old Towns in Europe.

Did you know? Estonia is one of the world's most digitally advanced nations — it's where Skype was born!

Geography

Estonia’s landscapes are diverse and peaceful:

  • Over 2,000 islands, including Saaremaa and Hiiumaa
  • Forests covering more than 50% of the country
  • Beautiful lakes like Lake Peipus and Lake Võrtsjärv
  • Lahemaa National Park with pristine nature and historic manor houses

Culture

Estonian culture is proud and creative:

  • Rich traditions of singing — the Song Festival is a UNESCO heritage event
  • Traditional foods like black bread, kama (grain mixture), and mulgikapsad (sauerkraut with pork)
  • A strong connection to folklore and nature
  • Modern digital innovations blended with centuries-old traditions

Tourism Highlights

Top places to explore in Estonia:

  • Wandering through Tallinn's medieval Old Town
  • Exploring Tartu, the intellectual and cultural heart of Estonia
  • Relaxing on the sandy beaches of Pärnu
  • Visiting the islands of Saaremaa and Kihnu
  • Nature adventures in Soomaa National Park's bog landscapes
