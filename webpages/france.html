About France

France is a Western European country known for its rich history, culture, and world-renowned cuisine. The capital city, Paris, is home to famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Geography

France shares borders with Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, Andorra, and Monaco. The country boasts diverse landscapes, including the Alps, the Pyrenees, and beautiful coastal regions.

Culture

France is a global center of art, fashion, and philosophy. It has influenced the world with its literature, music, and film. The country is also famous for its gastronomy, including wine, cheese, and pastries.
