Discover France

About France

France, known for its rich culture, historic landmarks, and world-renowned cuisine, is a country that has captivated visitors for centuries. From the romantic streets of Paris to the sun-soaked beaches of the French Riviera, France offers a diverse and unforgettable experience.

Did you know? France is the most visited country in the world and is home to 49 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including Mont-Saint-Michel and the Palace of Versailles.

Geography

France’s diverse landscapes include:

  • The Alps - home to Mont Blanc, Western Europe’s highest peak
  • The Loire Valley - known for its stunning châteaux and vineyards
  • The lavender fields of Provence
  • Atlantic beaches in Normandy and Brittany

Culture

French culture is world-famous for:

  • Haute cuisine and fine wines
  • Iconic art and museums like the Louvre
  • Fashion hubs like Paris, the "Capital of Fashion"
  • Traditional festivals such as Bastille Day and Cannes Film Festival

Tourism Highlights

Must-see places in France include:

  • The Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral in Paris
  • Château de Chambord in the Loire Valley
  • The stunning cliffs of Étretat
  • Provence’s vibrant villages and vineyards
  • The glamorous beaches of Nice and Saint-Tropez
