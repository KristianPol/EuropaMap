Discover Lithuania

About Lithuania

Lithuania, located on the southeastern shore of the Baltic Sea, is a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Once the largest country in Europe during the Middle Ages, Lithuania today offers a vibrant blend of medieval charm and modern innovation. Its capital, Vilnius, is known for its beautifully preserved Old Town and lively arts scene.

Did you know? Lithuania was the first Soviet republic to declare independence from the USSR in 1990!

Geography

Discover Lithuania’s diverse natural landscapes:

  • The Curonian Spit, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning dunes
  • Picturesque lakes and forests, especially in Aukštaitija National Park
  • The rolling hills and castles of Trakai
  • Endless meadows and river valleys

Culture

Lithuanian culture is proud, historic, and welcoming:

  • Deep-rooted folk traditions with unique songs and dances
  • Traditional dishes like cepelinai (potato dumplings) and šaltibarščiai (cold beet soup)
  • Rich Catholic heritage with numerous beautiful churches
  • Celebrations like Joninės (midsummer festival)

Tourism Highlights

Top sights and experiences in Lithuania:

  • The Old Town of Vilnius with its baroque architecture
  • Trakai Island Castle, a fairy-tale fortress on a lake
  • The Hill of Crosses, a powerful symbol of faith and resilience
  • Exploring Klaipėda and the Baltic Sea coast
  • Nature adventures in Dzūkija National Park
