Welcome to Serbia

About Serbia

Serbia is a landlocked country in Southeast Europe, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and beautiful landscapes. The capital city is Belgrade, which is one of the oldest cities in Europe and a vibrant cultural and economic hub.

Geography

Serbia is bordered by Hungary to the north, Romania to the northeast, Bulgaria to the southeast, North Macedonia to the south, Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina to the west, and Montenegro to the southwest. The country features a varied landscape, including fertile plains, rolling hills, and mountainous regions.

Culture

Serbia has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from both Eastern and Western civilizations. The country is known for its traditional music, dance, and festivals, as well as its contributions to literature, art, and science. Serbia is also famous for its Orthodox Christian monasteries and historic sites.

Tourism

Serbia is an emerging tourist destination, offering a mix of natural beauty, historic landmarks, and vibrant city life. Popular attractions include Belgrade Fortress, the Drina River House, the stunning Đavolja Varoš rock formations, and the vibrant nightlife of Belgrade.
