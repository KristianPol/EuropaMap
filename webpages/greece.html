Discover Greece

A Cradle of Civilization, Sun, and Sea

About Greece

Greece, located in southeastern Europe, is renowned for its rich history, ancient ruins, stunning islands, and contributions to democracy, philosophy, and the arts. It played a pivotal role in the development of Western civilization.

Its capital, Athens, is a vibrant city blending historical landmarks with modern life.

Did you know? Greece has over 6,000 islands, of which around 200 are inhabited!

Geography

Greece is defined by its mountainous mainland and picturesque islands:

  • Home to Mount Olympus, the mythical abode of the gods
  • Thousands of islands including Santorini, Mykonos, and Crete
  • A Mediterranean climate with long, sunny summers
  • Surrounded by the Aegean, Ionian, and Mediterranean seas

Culture

Greek culture is a blend of ancient heritage and vibrant traditions:

  • Birthplace of democracy, philosophy, and the Olympic Games
  • Rich tradition in theater, literature, and mythology
  • Famous cuisine with dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, and tzatziki
  • Strong Orthodox Christian heritage and iconic churches

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Greece include:

Acropolis of Athens

An iconic ancient citadel overlooking the city of Athens.

Santorini

Famous for its white-washed buildings and stunning sunsets.

Delphi

A historic sanctuary and oracle site of the ancient world.
