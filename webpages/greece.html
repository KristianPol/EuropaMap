Welcome to Greece

About Greece

Greece is a country in Southeastern Europe, known for its ancient history, stunning islands, and rich cultural heritage. The capital city is Athens, home to historical landmarks like the Acropolis and Parthenon.

Geography

Greece is bordered by Albania, North Macedonia, Bulgaria, and Turkey. It features thousands of islands, including Crete, Santorini, and Mykonos. The country is surrounded by the Aegean, Ionian, and Mediterranean Seas.

Culture

Greece has a deep cultural heritage with influences from its ancient civilization. The country is known for its mythology, philosophy, and Olympic Games. Greek cuisine, including dishes like souvlaki, moussaka, and baklava, is famous worldwide.

Tourism

Greece is a top tourist destination, offering breathtaking landscapes, historical ruins, and beautiful beaches. Popular attractions include Santorini, the Acropolis, Meteora, and the ancient city of Delphi.
