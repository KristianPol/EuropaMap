Discover Greece

About Greece

Greece, located in southeastern Europe, is a country steeped in ancient history and breathtaking natural beauty. Known as the cradle of Western civilization, Greece gave the world democracy, philosophy, and the Olympic Games. Its capital, Athens, is famous for iconic landmarks like the Parthenon atop the Acropolis.

Did you know? Greece has more than 6,000 islands and islets scattered across the Aegean and Ionian Seas!

Geography

Greece's landscapes are incredibly diverse:

  • The whitewashed villages and blue-domed churches of Santorini
  • The rugged peaks of Mount Olympus, mythological home of the Greek gods
  • Stunning beaches along the Peloponnese coast
  • The lush, green landscapes of Corfu and Crete

Culture

Greek culture is rich, colorful, and influential worldwide:

  • Traditional Greek music with instruments like the bouzouki
  • Delicious cuisine featuring moussaka, souvlaki, and baklava
  • Vibrant festivals celebrating local saints and historic events
  • Hospitality, or "philoxenia," is a deeply ingrained value

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions in Greece include:

  • The Acropolis of Athens and the Parthenon
  • The monasteries perched atop the rocks of Meteora
  • The ancient ruins of Delphi, once considered the center of the world
  • Island hopping through the Cyclades, including Mykonos and Naxos
  • Exploring the ancient palace of Knossos in Crete
