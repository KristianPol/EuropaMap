About Italy

Italy is a European country known for its rich history, iconic architecture, world-class cuisine, and stunning natural landscapes. From the Roman Empire to the Renaissance, Italy has played a central role in shaping Western civilization.

Geography

Located in Southern Europe, Italy is a peninsula surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea. It shares borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia. It also includes the islands of Sicily and Sardinia, and features diverse geography from the Alps to coastal plains.

Culture

Italian culture is renowned for its contributions to art, music, fashion, and cuisine. Cities like Rome, Florence, and Venice are home to some of the world's greatest masterpieces and historic sites. Italian traditions are deeply rooted in family, food, and festivals.
