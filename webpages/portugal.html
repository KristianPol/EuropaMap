Discover Portugal

About Portugal

Portugal, located on the Iberian Peninsula, is a country of rich maritime heritage, charming historic cities, golden beaches, and vibrant culture. Its capital, Lisbon, is a colorful city known for its hills, trams, and soulful fado music.

Did you know? Portugal is the oldest country in Europe with the same defined borders, and it was once the world’s leading maritime power during the Age of Discoveries.

Geography

Portugal’s varied landscapes include:

  • The Douro Valley - famous for its terraced vineyards
  • The Algarve - stunning coastline with golden beaches and cliffs
  • Serra da Estrela - the highest mountain range in mainland Portugal
  • The Azores and Madeira - beautiful Atlantic island groups

Culture

Portugal’s traditions are rich and diverse:

  • Fado music - recognized as UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage
  • Traditional tiles (azulejos) decorating buildings and streets
  • Delicious cuisine featuring bacalhau, pastel de nata, and hearty stews
  • Festivals like Festa de São João in Porto and Carnival in Madeira

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions in Portugal include:

  • Belém Tower and Jerónimos Monastery in Lisbon
  • Historic city of Porto and its famous port wine cellars
  • Sintra’s fairy-tale palaces and castles
  • Óbidos - a beautiful medieval walled town
  • Madeira’s Laurisilva Forest, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
