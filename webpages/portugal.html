Welcome to Portugal

About Portugal

Portugal is a Southern European country on the Iberian Peninsula, bordering Spain. It is known for its rich maritime history, stunning coastline, and vibrant culture.

Geography

Portugal features diverse landscapes, from the beautiful beaches of the Algarve to the rolling vineyards of the Douro Valley and the historic cities of Lisbon and Porto.

Culture

Portugal has a rich cultural heritage, famous for its Fado music, delicious cuisine including pastéis de nata, and its historic explorers like Vasco da Gama.

Tourism

Portugal is a top travel destination, offering breathtaking sights such as the Tower of Belém, the Pena Palace in Sintra, and the scenic cliffs of Lagos.
