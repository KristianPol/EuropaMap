Discover Kosovo

About Kosovo

Kosovo, a landlocked country in Southeast Europe, is known for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. The capital, Pristina, is the heart of the country, offering a mix of modernity and traditional charm. Kosovo’s history is deeply influenced by various cultures and empires, with a unique blend of Albanian, Ottoman, and Slavic heritage visible throughout the country.

Did you know? Kosovo is home to the UNESCO-listed Patriarchate of Peć, one of the most important medieval Serbian Orthodox monasteries!

Geography

Kosovo’s landscape is a blend of rugged mountains, fertile plains, and beautiful rivers:

  • The Sharr Mountains, which border Kosovo and North Macedonia, offer stunning views and hiking trails
  • The Rugova Canyon, one of the most impressive natural formations in the Balkans
  • The Lepenac River, which flows through Kosovo’s central region
  • Germia Park in Pristina, a natural urban park with hiking trails and panoramic views of the capital

Culture

Kosovo’s culture is a beautiful mix of different traditions, influenced by its diverse past:

  • The Albanian language and traditions are central to Kosovo’s identity
  • The rich history of Ottoman influence can be seen in the architecture, food, and traditions
  • Famous for its folk music, dance, and celebrations, like the traditional wedding celebrations and festive gatherings
  • Delicious Kosovo cuisine, including dishes like flija (layered pancake dish) and pasul (bean stew)

Tourism Highlights

Some of Kosovo’s top attractions include:

  • The UNESCO-listed Patriarchate of Peć Monastery, a historical and religious landmark
  • The city of Gjakova, known for its historic Old Bazaar and stunning Ottoman architecture
  • The beautiful town of Prizren, with its mix of Islamic and Christian heritage, charming old town, and stunning fortress
  • The Brezovica ski resort in the Sharr Mountains, a great destination for winter sports
  • The Kosovo Museum in Pristina, offering insight into the region’s rich history and culture
