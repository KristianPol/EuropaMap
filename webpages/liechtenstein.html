Welcome to Liechtenstein

About Liechtenstein

Liechtenstein is a small, alpine country located between Switzerland and Austria. It is one of the world’s smallest and richest nations, known for its beautiful mountains, strong financial sector, and medieval heritage.

Geography

With a land area of just over 160 square kilometers, Liechtenstein is a landlocked principality nestled in the Alps. It features lush valleys, mountainous terrain, and the Rhine River marking its western border with Switzerland.

Culture

Liechtenstein has a rich cultural tradition influenced by its German-speaking heritage. It celebrates its national day on August 15 with festivals, fireworks, and an open reception at Vaduz Castle hosted by the royal family.

Tourism

Popular tourist attractions include Vaduz Castle, the Gutenberg Castle in Balzers, scenic hiking trails, and museums like the Kunstmuseum. Visitors are drawn to the country’s peaceful charm, natural beauty, and clean environment.
