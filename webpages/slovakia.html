Discover Slovakia

About Slovakia

Slovakia, located in the heart of Europe, is a land of majestic mountains, medieval castles, and vibrant folk traditions. With its rich history and welcoming people, Slovakia offers a unique blend of cultural and natural wonders.

Did you know? Slovakia is home to more than 6,000 caves and the highest number of castles and châteaux per capita in the world!

Geography

Slovakia's landscapes are truly breathtaking:

  • The High Tatras — the smallest high mountain range in the world
  • Beautiful caves like Dobšinská Ice Cave (UNESCO)
  • The Danube River flowing along the southern border
  • Picturesque valleys and vineyards of southern Slovakia

Culture

Slovak culture is proud and lively:

  • Traditional folk music with bagpipes and cimbalom
  • Colorful costumes and folklore festivals
  • Delicious cuisine including bryndzové halušky and kapustnica
  • Rich traditions of woodcraft, pottery, and embroidery

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions in Slovakia include:

  • Bratislava’s Old Town and Bratislava Castle
  • Spiš Castle (one of the largest castles in Europe)
  • Banská Štiavnica’s historic mining town (UNESCO)
  • Vlkolínec — a perfectly preserved folk village (UNESCO)
  • The Slovak Paradise National Park with scenic trails and ladders
