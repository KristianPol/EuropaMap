Discover Poland

About Poland

Poland, located in Central Europe, is a country of rich history, resilient spirit, and stunning natural landscapes. From medieval old towns to modern cities, Poland offers a deep connection to its past and a vibrant future.

Did you know? Poland is home to 17 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the historic centers of Kraków and Warsaw!

Geography

Poland’s landscapes are wonderfully diverse:

  • The Tatra Mountains along the southern border
  • Masurian Lake District, perfect for water sports and relaxation
  • The Baltic Sea coastline with sandy beaches
  • Białowieża Forest, one of Europe's last primeval forests

Culture

Poland has a proud and rich cultural heritage:

  • Traditional folk dances like the Polonaise and Mazurka
  • World-famous composers like Frédéric Chopin
  • Traditional dishes such as pierogi, bigos, and żurek
  • Festivals like Wianki and St. John's Night celebrations

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Poland include:

  • Wawel Castle and Old Town Kraków
  • Warsaw’s Royal Castle and vibrant old town
  • Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum
  • Gdańsk's picturesque waterfront and historical center
  • Zakopane and the Tatra Mountains for skiing and hiking
