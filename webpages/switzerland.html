Welcome to Switzerland

About Switzerland

Switzerland is a landlocked country in Central Europe, known for its breathtaking alpine landscapes, strong economy, and high quality of life. The capital city, Bern, is known for its medieval old town, while Zurich and Geneva are global financial hubs.

Geography

Switzerland is famous for its majestic Alps, picturesque lakes, and lush valleys. It shares borders with Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein.

Culture

Switzerland has a diverse cultural heritage influenced by German, French, and Italian traditions. It is known for its precision engineering, chocolate, cheese, and world-famous neutrality.

Tourism

Tourists visit Switzerland for its stunning mountain resorts, including Zermatt and Interlaken, as well as historic cities and scenic train journeys like the Glacier Express.
