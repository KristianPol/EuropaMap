Discover Denmark

About Denmark

Denmark, a Scandinavian gem, is known for its modern cities, medieval castles, and Viking heritage. The capital, Copenhagen, combines the charm of its old town with cutting-edge design and innovation. Denmark is also famous for its high quality of life and progressive social systems.

Did you know? Denmark is the birthplace of LEGO, and the Danish people consistently rank among the happiest in the world!

Geography

Denmark’s geography is diverse, with flat landscapes, sandy beaches, and scenic coastal islands:

  • The Jutland Peninsula, bordered by the North Sea and the Baltic Sea
  • The stunning island of Zealand, home to Copenhagen
  • The picturesque coastlines of the Danish Riviera
  • Hundested and Skagen, known for their sandy shores and fishing ports

Culture

Denmark has a rich cultural heritage, blending traditional practices with modern innovation:

  • Denmark is home to the famous Danish design, including furniture, architecture, and fashion
  • The concept of “hygge,” representing coziness and well-being
  • Traditional Danish cuisine, such as smørrebrød (open-faced sandwiches) and Danish pastries
  • The renowned Danish fairy tales of Hans Christian Andersen

Tourism Highlights

Must-visit places in Denmark include:

  • Nyhavn and the Tivoli Gardens in Copenhagen
  • The majestic Kronborg Castle, the setting for Shakespeare’s Hamlet
  • The Viking Ship Museum in Roskilde
  • Legoland Billund, the birthplace of the LEGO toy
  • Skagen’s art galleries and beaches
