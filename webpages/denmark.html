Discover Denmark

A Land of Vikings, Design, and Hygge

About Denmark

Denmark, a Nordic country in Northern Europe, consists of the Jutland Peninsula and numerous islands. It is known for its high quality of life, modern architecture, and deep-rooted Viking history.

The capital city, Copenhagen, is a vibrant hub of culture, cuisine, and sustainable living. Denmark consistently ranks as one of the happiest countries in the world.

Did you know? Denmark is home to the oldest monarchy in Europe and the original home of LEGO!

Geography

Denmark is characterized by its flat landscapes, coastal beauty, and islands:

  • Surrounded by the North Sea and the Baltic Sea
  • Made up of over 400 islands, including Zealand and Funen
  • Known for its offshore wind farms and green energy
  • Home to natural parks and scenic coastal trails

Culture

Danish culture blends history, simplicity, and modern design:

  • The monarchy is an important symbol of Danish heritage
  • Renowned for minimalist and functional design
  • Danish cuisine includes smørrebrød and New Nordic food
  • "Hygge" – the cozy Danish lifestyle philosophy

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Denmark include:

Nyhavn

Colorful harbor district in Copenhagen filled with history and charm.

LEGOLAND Billund

The original LEGOLAND theme park—fun for all ages.

Kronborg Castle

UNESCO site and setting of Shakespeare’s Hamlet.
