About Denmark

Denmark is a Scandinavian country known for its rich history, modern design, and high standard of living. The capital city, Copenhagen, is famous for its colorful waterfront, historic castles, and vibrant culture.

Geography

Denmark consists of the Jutland Peninsula and over 400 islands, with a coastline stretching along the North and Baltic Seas. It shares a land border with Germany and is connected to Sweden via the Ã˜resund Bridge.

Culture

Danish culture is known for its emphasis on "hygge" (coziness), strong design traditions, and contributions to literature and philosophy. Denmark is also famous for its Viking heritage and monarchy.

Tourism

Tourists visit Denmark for attractions such as the Tivoli Gardens, the Little Mermaid statue, and the scenic coastal towns. The country is also home to world-class museums and modern architecture.
