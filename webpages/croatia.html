Welcome to Croatia

About Croatia

Croatia is a beautiful country in Southeast Europe known for its stunning Adriatic coastline, historic cities, and breathtaking national parks. The capital, Zagreb, blends medieval and modern charm.

Geography

Croatia boasts over a thousand islands, stunning beaches, and famous natural landmarks like Plitvice Lakes and Krka National Parks. It shares borders with Slovenia, Hungary, Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Montenegro.

Culture

Croatia has a rich cultural heritage, known for its folk traditions, music, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine. The country is also home to the famous Dubrovnik city walls, which inspired locations in Game of Thrones.

Tourism

Tourists flock to Croatia for its picturesque coastal towns like Dubrovnik and Split, stunning islands like Hvar, and UNESCO World Heritage sites. The country is also famous for its sailing, wine, and festivals.
