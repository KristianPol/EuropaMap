Welcome to the Netherlands

About the Netherlands

The Netherlands, also known as Holland, is a country in Northwestern Europe known for its flat landscape, canals, windmills, and tulip fields. The capital city, Amsterdam, is famous for its historic architecture, museums, and vibrant culture.

Geography

The Netherlands is bordered by Germany to the east, Belgium to the south, and the North Sea to the northwest. Much of the country lies below sea level, protected by an extensive system of dikes and water management structures.

Culture

The Netherlands has a rich cultural heritage, with contributions in art, literature, and music. It is home to famous painters such as Rembrandt and Van Gogh. Dutch cuisine includes stroopwafels, cheese, and raw herring.

Tourism

The Netherlands attracts millions of visitors each year, with popular attractions including the Anne Frank House, Keukenhof Gardens, Rijksmuseum, and the scenic canals of Amsterdam. The country is also known for its cycling-friendly infrastructure.
