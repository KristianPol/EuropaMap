Discover Netherlands

About Netherlands

The Netherlands, located in Western Europe, is a country renowned for its flat landscape, extensive canal systems, colorful tulip fields, windmills, and vibrant cities. Amsterdam, its capital, is famous for its historic houses, artistic heritage, and lively culture.

Did you know? About a third of the Netherlands lies below sea level, protected by an intricate system of dikes and canals!

Geography

The Netherlands' unique geography includes:

  • Extensive polders — land reclaimed from the sea
  • Iconic tulip fields, especially in the Keukenhof Gardens
  • Picturesque waterways and canals in cities like Amsterdam and Utrecht
  • Coastal dunes and the Wadden Sea islands

Culture

The Netherlands boasts a rich and open culture:

  • Masterpieces from painters like Rembrandt and Van Gogh
  • Celebrations like King's Day (Koningsdag)
  • Traditional foods such as stroopwafels, herring, and cheese
  • A cycling culture with more bikes than people!

Tourism Highlights

Top places and experiences to enjoy:

  • Exploring the canals and museums of Amsterdam
  • Visiting the windmills at Kinderdijk (UNESCO site)
  • Seeing the vibrant tulip displays at Keukenhof
  • Touring the medieval city of Utrecht
  • Discovering Rotterdam’s modern architecture
