Welcome to Hungary

About Hungary

Hungary is a landlocked country in Central Europe known for its rich history, architectural wonders, and thermal spas. The capital, Budapest, is famous for its stunning Parliament building and vibrant nightlife.

Geography

Hungary features rolling plains, beautiful lakes such as Lake Balaton, and the iconic Danube River that flows through Budapest. It shares borders with Austria, Slovakia, Ukraine, Romania, Serbia, Croatia, and Slovenia.

Culture

Hungary has a deep cultural heritage, renowned for its folk music, traditional dance, and famous cuisine, including goulash and chimney cake. Hungarian literature and classical music also have a significant influence.

Tourism

Tourists visit Hungary to experience the breathtaking Buda Castle, the historic city of Eger, and the scenic beauty of Lake Balaton. The country is also known for its world-famous thermal baths and vibrant festivals.
