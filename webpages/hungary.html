Discover Hungary

About Hungary

Hungary, located in Central Europe, is a country with a rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and a fascinating history. The capital, Budapest, is often referred to as the "Paris of the East" for its stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and historical significance.

Did you know? Hungary is home to the oldest metro line in mainland Europe, and the famous Hungarian language, known for its unique structure, is unrelated to most other European languages!

Geography

Hungary features diverse and scenic landscapes:

  • The Danube River, flowing through the heart of Budapest
  • The Great Hungarian Plain, a vast flatland offering a unique view of the countryside
  • The Buda Hills and the picturesque Tokaj wine region
  • Lake Balaton, Central Europe's largest freshwater lake

Culture

Hungarian culture is full of rich traditions and unique elements:

  • Famous for its classical music, with composers like Franz Liszt and Béla Bartók
  • Hungarian cuisine, including goulash, lángos, and chimney cake
  • Rich tradition of folk art, including embroidery and pottery
  • The Hungarian language, one of the oldest and most unique in Europe

Tourism Highlights

Must-see destinations in Hungary include:

  • The stunning Buda Castle and Fisherman’s Bastion in Budapest
  • Thermal baths, such as the Széchenyi and Gellért Baths in Budapest
  • The historic town of Eger and its medieval castle
  • The vineyards and wineries of the Tokaj region (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • The beautiful shores of Lake Balaton and the surrounding towns
