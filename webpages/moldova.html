Welcome to Moldova

About Moldova

Moldova is a small landlocked country in Eastern Europe, known for its rolling vineyards, rich cultural heritage, and hospitable people. The capital, Chișinău, is the country’s largest city and economic center.

Geography

Moldova is characterized by its picturesque countryside, fertile plains, and scenic rivers, including the Dniester. It shares borders with Romania and Ukraine.

Culture

Moldovan culture is influenced by Romanian, Russian, and Ottoman traditions. The country is famous for its folk music, dance, and winemaking, with some of the world's largest wine cellars.

Tourism

Tourists visit Moldova for its excellent wines, the historic Orheiul Vechi Monastery, and charming villages. The country is also home to vibrant festivals celebrating its rich traditions.
