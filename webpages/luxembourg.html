About Luxembourg

Luxembourg is a small yet wealthy country in Western Europe, known for its strong economy, medieval castles, and multicultural society. The capital, Luxembourg City, is a major financial hub with a blend of historical and modern architecture.

Geography

Luxembourg is a landlocked country bordered by Belgium, France, and Germany. It features rolling hills, dense forests, and scenic river valleys, making it a picturesque destination.

Culture

Luxembourg has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its multilingual population and neighboring countries. It is known for its festivals, gastronomy, and a strong sense of European identity.
