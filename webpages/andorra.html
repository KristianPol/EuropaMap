Discover Andorra

The Hidden Jewel of the Pyrenees

About Andorra

Andorra is a small, landlocked country nestled between France and Spain in the Pyrenees mountains. Known for its picturesque landscapes, ski resorts, and duty-free shopping, Andorra offers a peaceful and scenic retreat for travelers.

Despite its small size, Andorra boasts a rich cultural heritage, beautiful Romanesque architecture, and a unique political system with two co-princes as heads of state.

Did you know? Andorra is one of the few countries in the world with Catalan as its official language!

Geography

Andorra’s stunning geography is shaped by the Pyrenees:

  • Surrounded by dramatic mountain ranges and valleys
  • Famous for ski resorts like Grandvalira and Vallnord
  • Home to lush forests, alpine meadows, and clear mountain streams
  • Popular destination for hiking and mountain biking in the summer

Culture

Andorran culture is a blend of Catalan roots and Pyrenean tradition:

  • Catalan is the official language, with French and Spanish widely spoken
  • Traditional music and folk dances are celebrated in local festivals
  • Many Romanesque churches dot the landscape, reflecting Andorra’s deep Christian heritage
  • Cuisine blends French, Catalan, and Spanish influences

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions and experiences in Andorra include:

Vall de Núria

A beautiful valley perfect for skiing in winter and hiking in summer.

Sant Joan de Caselles

One of Andorra’s finest Romanesque churches, dating back to the 11th century.

Caldea Spa

One of Europe's largest spas, known for thermal waters and stunning architecture.
