Discover Czech Republic

A Fairytale Heart of Europe

About the Czech Republic

The Czech Republic, located in Central Europe, is known for its medieval towns, castles, and cultural heritage. Its capital, Prague, is often called “the City of a Hundred Spires” and is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe.

Historically part of Bohemia, Moravia, and Silesia, the country boasts a rich legacy of Gothic, Renaissance, and Baroque architecture.

Did you know? Prague Castle is the largest ancient castle in the world!

Geography

The Czech Republic features rolling hills, river valleys, and historical towns:

  • Prague on the Vltava River – a cultural and architectural marvel
  • Krkonoše Mountains – popular for hiking and skiing
  • Bohemian Forest – a large, biodiverse area with lakes and woodlands
  • Morava River Valley – fertile and historically significant

Culture

The Czech Republic boasts a vibrant cultural life rooted in music, literature, and folklore:

  • Home to composers like Antonín Dvořák and Bedřich Smetana
  • Beer culture with famous brands like Pilsner and Budvar
  • A tradition of puppetry and classical theater
  • Cuisine features hearty meals like goulash, dumplings, and svíčková

Tourism Highlights

Top destinations and sights in the Czech Republic:

Prague

Famous for its Old Town, Charles Bridge, and astronomical clock.

Český Krumlov

A fairy-tale town with a castle overlooking a winding river.

Karlovy Vary

A spa town famous for hot springs and elegant architecture.
