Discover Ukraine

About Ukraine

Ukraine, located in Eastern Europe, is known for its vast landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and resilient spirit. From the bustling streets of Kyiv to the serene beauty of the Carpathian Mountains, Ukraine offers a unique blend of history, tradition, and nature.

Did you know? Ukraine is the largest country entirely in Europe and is famous for its fertile farmlands, often called the "breadbasket of Europe."

Geography

Ukraine’s beautiful geography includes:

  • The Carpathian Mountains - perfect for hiking and winter sports
  • The Black Sea coastline with resorts like Odesa
  • The Dnipro River - one of Europe's major rivers
  • The vast steppes and golden sunflower fields

Culture

Ukrainian culture is full of traditions and pride:

  • Traditional dances like Hopak
  • Vyshyvanka - beautifully embroidered shirts
  • Rich folklore, music, and poetry
  • Delicious cuisine including borscht, varenyky, and holubtsi

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions in Ukraine include:

  • Kyiv’s Saint Sophia Cathedral and Pechersk Lavra
  • Lviv’s charming Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Odesa’s famous Potemkin Stairs and beaches
  • The Tunnel of Love near Klevan
  • Chernobyl Exclusion Zone tours
