About Belgium

Belgium is a country in Western Europe known for its medieval towns, Renaissance architecture, and as the headquarters of the European Union. The capital city, Brussels, is famous for its historic sites and vibrant culture.

Geography

Belgium shares borders with France, Germany, Luxembourg, and the Netherlands. The country features coastal plains, rolling hills, and the Ardennes forest in the south.
