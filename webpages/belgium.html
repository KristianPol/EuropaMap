Discover Belgium

About Belgium

Belgium, located in Western Europe, is known for its medieval towns, Renaissance architecture, and beautiful landscapes. Brussels, the capital, is the heart of European politics and home to the European Union. Belgium also boasts a rich history, fine chocolates, and world-famous beers.

Did you know? Belgium is home to over 1,000 different types of beer!

Geography

Belgium's diverse geography includes:

  • The picturesque Ardennes forest in the south
  • The flat, coastal region with sandy beaches along the North Sea
  • The rolling hills and vineyards of the Walloon region
  • Famous rivers like the Meuse and Scheldt

Culture

Belgium’s culture is rich and varied, shaped by its French, Dutch, and German influences:

  • Belgian chocolates are world-renowned, especially pralines
  • The famous “Flemish Masters” such as Van Eyck and Rubens
  • Delicious cuisine including moules-frites, waffles, and stoofvlees
  • Festivals like the Carnival of Binche, a UNESCO recognized event

Tourism Highlights

Some must-see attractions in Belgium include:

  • The historic Grand Place in Brussels
  • The medieval city of Bruges with its canals and cobblestone streets
  • The Battle of Waterloo site, where Napoleon was defeated
  • The stunning town of Ghent with its castles and art galleries
  • Exploring the beautiful town of Antwerp, known for its diamonds
