Welcome to Romania

About Romania

Romania is a country in Eastern Europe, known for its medieval castles, scenic landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. The capital city is Bucharest, often called the "Little Paris" for its architecture and vibrant lifestyle.

Geography

Romania is bordered by Ukraine to the north, Bulgaria to the south, Serbia to the southwest, Hungary to the west, and Moldova to the east. The country features the Carpathian Mountains, vast forests, and the Danube River, which flows into the Black Sea.

Culture

Romania has a deep cultural heritage influenced by its Latin roots, Byzantine traditions, and neighboring countries. The country is famous for its folklore, traditional music, and painted monasteries. Romanian cuisine is also well known, featuring dishes like sarmale, mici, and mămăligă.

Tourism

Romania is a popular tourist destination with attractions such as Bran Castle (often associated with Dracula), the Transfagarasan Highway, the Danube Delta, and the medieval city of Sibiu. The country is also known for its stunning landscapes and warm hospitality.
