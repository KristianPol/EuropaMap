Discover Norway

About Norway

Norway, located in Northern Europe, is a country of stunning natural beauty, famous for its dramatic fjords, majestic mountains, and vibrant coastal towns. Oslo, the capital, blends modern architecture with deep-rooted Viking heritage, while offering a high standard of living and cultural richness.

Did you know? Norway is home to the world's longest road tunnel — the Lærdal Tunnel — stretching over 24 kilometers!

Geography

Norway’s landscapes are among the most breathtaking on Earth:

  • Majestic fjords like Geirangerfjord and Nærøyfjord (both UNESCO World Heritage Sites)
  • The rugged peaks of the Lofoten Islands
  • The Arctic wilderness of Svalbard
  • Endless forests and lakes in the interior

Culture

Norwegian culture is rich and nature-oriented:

  • A strong maritime and Viking heritage
  • Celebrations like Constitution Day (May 17th)
  • Traditional dishes like rakfisk, brunost (brown cheese), and seafood specialties
  • Passion for skiing and outdoor activities in all seasons

Tourism Highlights

Must-see attractions in Norway:

  • Oslo’s Opera House and Vigeland Sculpture Park
  • Bergen’s colorful Bryggen Wharf (UNESCO site)
  • The breathtaking Atlantic Ocean Road
  • Chasing the Northern Lights in Tromsø
  • Hiking to Trolltunga and Preikestolen (Pulpit Rock)
