About Norway

Norway is a Scandinavian country in Northern Europe, known for its breathtaking fjords, rugged coastline, and stunning natural beauty. The capital city, Oslo, is a vibrant hub of culture, history, and modern design.

Geography

Norway is famous for its dramatic landscapes, including deep fjords, towering mountains, and Arctic tundra. It shares borders with Sweden, Finland, and Russia, and has a long coastline along the North Atlantic Ocean.

Culture

Norway has a rich cultural heritage, with traditions rooted in Viking history, folklore, and Sami indigenous culture. The country is also known for its contributions to art, literature (like Henrik Ibsen), and music (such as Edvard Grieg).
