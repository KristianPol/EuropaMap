Welcome to Latvia

About Latvia

Latvia is a beautiful Baltic country in Northern Europe known for its diverse culture, medieval architecture, and stunning natural landscapes. The capital, Riga, is famous for its well-preserved Old Town and Art Nouveau architecture.

Geography

Latvia features vast forests, pristine beaches along the Baltic Sea, and thousands of lakes and rivers. It shares borders with Estonia, Russia, Belarus, and Lithuania. The country is known for its four distinct seasons and picturesque countryside.

Culture

Latvia has a rich cultural heritage with traditional folk songs (dainas), midsummer celebrations (Jāņi), and unique cuisine featuring rye bread, grey peas, and smoked fish. The country has a strong choral tradition and hosts one of the largest song festivals in the world.

Tourism

Tourists visit Latvia to explore the historic Riga Old Town (a UNESCO site), the stunning beaches of Jurmala, and the medieval castles like Rundāle Palace. The country offers excellent opportunities for nature tourism, including birdwatching in Ķemeri National Park and hiking in Gauja National Park.
