Discover Latvia

About Latvia

Latvia, nestled along the Baltic Sea between Estonia and Lithuania, is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities. Riga, the capital, is famous for its stunning Art Nouveau architecture and charming Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Did you know? Latvia is often called the "Land of Blue Lakes and Green Forests," with forests covering over half the country!

Geography

Latvia offers a wide variety of natural beauty:

  • Vast forests and hundreds of crystal-clear lakes
  • The scenic Gauja National Park, known as the "Switzerland of Latvia"
  • Beautiful sandy beaches along the Baltic Sea, especially in Jūrmala
  • Charming rivers and picturesque countryside

Culture

Latvian culture is colorful and deeply rooted in tradition:

  • Rich folklore and traditional dainas (folk songs)
  • Traditional crafts such as weaving and pottery
  • Delicious dishes like grey peas with bacon and rye bread
  • The National Song and Dance Festival, one of the largest choral events in the world

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions and experiences in Latvia include:

  • Riga’s Old Town and Central Market
  • Gauja National Park and the medieval town of Cēsis
  • Relaxing in the seaside resort town of Jūrmala
  • Exploring Rundāle Palace, a masterpiece of Baroque architecture
  • Hiking and wildlife spotting in Kemeri National Park
