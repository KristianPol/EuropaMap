Discover Finland

About Finland

Finland, known as the "Land of a Thousand Lakes," is a stunning country in Northern Europe characterized by breathtaking nature, innovative design, and a high standard of living. Helsinki, the vibrant capital, offers a mix of modern architecture and traditional culture. Finland is also famous for being the home of Santa Claus and the magical Northern Lights.

Did you know? Finland consistently ranks among the happiest countries in the world according to the World Happiness Report!

Geography

Finland’s landscape is peaceful and majestic:

  • Over 188,000 lakes and dense forests covering much of the country
  • The Arctic Lapland region, home to the indigenous Sámi people
  • The scenic archipelagos along the southern and western coasts
  • National parks like Nuuksio and Koli offering pristine wilderness

Culture

Finnish culture is unique and nature-connected:

  • Sauna culture is deeply rooted, with more saunas than cars!
  • Innovative design traditions, from Marimekko textiles to Alvar Aalto’s architecture
  • Traditional foods like rye bread, karjalanpiirakka (Karelian pasty), and salmon soup
  • Festivals like Vappu (spring festival) and Juhannus (Midsummer celebration)

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions and experiences in Finland:

  • Exploring the stylish city of Helsinki
  • Visiting Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi
  • Experiencing the Northern Lights in Lapland
  • Cruising around the Turku Archipelago
  • Hiking and relaxing in the national parks
