About Finland

Finland is a Nordic country in Northern Europe, known for its stunning natural landscapes, high quality of life, and innovative technology. The capital city, Helsinki, is a vibrant cultural hub with a mix of modern and neoclassical architecture.

Geography

Finland is often called the "Land of a Thousand Lakes" because of its 188,000 lakes and vast forests. It shares borders with Sweden, Norway, and Russia, and has a long coastline along the Baltic Sea.

Culture

Finland has a rich cultural heritage, with traditions rooted in its Nordic and Sami indigenous cultures. The country is known for its sauna culture, design (like Marimekko and Alvar Aalto), and contributions to music (such as Jean Sibelius).
