Discover Finland

The Land of a Thousand Lakes, Northern Lights, and Saunas

About Finland

Finland, located in Northern Europe, is known for its stunning natural landscapes, high quality of life, and forward-thinking society. It's one of the happiest and most sustainable countries in the world.

The capital, Helsinki, offers a unique mix of Scandinavian design, modern architecture, and a vibrant waterfront culture.

Did you know? Finland has over 3 million saunas—more than one per household!

Geography

Finland’s natural environment is peaceful, clean, and full of wonder:

  • Known as the "Land of a Thousand Lakes"
  • Over 70% of Finland is covered by forest
  • Cold winters with snowy wonderlands in Lapland
  • Home to fells and wilderness areas in the north

Culture

Finnish culture is built around nature, equality, and innovation:

  • Sauna culture is central to Finnish life
  • High literacy rates and strong public libraries
  • Innovators in education, design, and technology
  • Outdoor activities like skiing and ice swimming are common

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Finland include:

Helsinki

The capital city with stunning architecture, museums, and design districts.

Lapland

Home of Santa Claus, Northern Lights, and Arctic adventures.

Lake Saimaa

Finland’s largest lake—perfect for boating, relaxation, and spotting rare seals.
