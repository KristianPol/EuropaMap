Welcome to Sweden

About Sweden

Sweden is a Scandinavian country in Northern Europe, known for its stunning natural landscapes, innovative design, and high quality of life. The capital city, Stockholm, is built on 14 islands and is famous for its historic architecture, museums, and vibrant culture.

Geography

Sweden is characterized by its vast forests, thousands of lakes, and a long coastline along the Baltic Sea. It shares borders with Norway, Finland, and is connected to Denmark by the Öresund Bridge.

Culture

Sweden has a rich cultural heritage, known for its contributions to music (like ABBA and Swedish House Mafia), literature (such as Astrid Lindgren), and design (IKEA and minimalist aesthetics). The country also celebrates traditional festivals like Midsummer and Lucia.

Tourism

Sweden is a popular tourist destination, offering attractions such as Stockholm's Gamla Stan (Old Town), the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, the Northern Lights in Swedish Lapland, and the picturesque archipelagoes along its coast.
