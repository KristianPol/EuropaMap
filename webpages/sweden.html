Discover Sweden

About Sweden

Sweden, located in Northern Europe, is a country known for its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and high quality of life. Stockholm, the stylish capital built across 14 islands, is a hub of innovation, design, and cultural heritage. Sweden's balance between modernity and nature makes it a truly remarkable destination.

Did you know? Sweden has a right called "Allemansrätten," meaning everyone can roam freely through nature, respecting the environment and private property.

Geography

Sweden’s natural beauty is incredibly diverse:

  • The serene forests and lakes of central Sweden
  • The snowy peaks of Swedish Lapland
  • The picturesque islands of the Stockholm Archipelago
  • The dramatic cliffs and coastlines of Gotland

Culture

Swedish culture is progressive yet deeply rooted in tradition:

  • Famous for fika — the cultural institution of a coffee break with pastries
  • Celebrations like Midsummer, with traditional flower crowns and dances
  • Delicious dishes such as meatballs, gravlax, and cinnamon buns
  • Strong traditions in design and music, from IKEA to ABBA

Tourism Highlights

Top attractions and experiences in Sweden:

  • Exploring Stockholm’s Gamla Stan (Old Town)
  • Seeing the Northern Lights in Abisko National Park
  • Visiting the medieval city of Visby on Gotland
  • Discovering the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi
  • Hiking in the scenic Sarek National Park
