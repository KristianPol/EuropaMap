Welcome to Russia

About Russia

Russia is the largest country in the world, spanning Eastern Europe and northern Asia. The capital city, Moscow, is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Kremlin and Red Square.

Geography

Russia shares borders with multiple countries, including China, Ukraine, and Kazakhstan. The country features diverse landscapes, from tundra and forests to mountains and vast plains.

Culture

Russia has a rich cultural heritage, known for its literature, ballet, and classical music. Famous Russian authors include Tolstoy and Dostoevsky, while the Bolshoi Ballet is world-renowned.

Tourism

Russia attracts visitors with its historic cities such as Saint Petersburg and Moscow. Popular attractions include the Hermitage Museum, Lake Baikal, and the Trans-Siberian Railway.
