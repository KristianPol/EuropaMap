Discover Russia

About Russia

Russia, the largest country in the world, spans across two continents and encompasses a wealth of diverse cultures, landscapes, and history. From the vibrant streets of Moscow to the majestic Siberian wilderness, Russia is home to some of the world’s most iconic landmarks and cultural heritage.

Did you know? Russia's Trans-Siberian Railway is the longest railway line in the world, stretching over 9,000 kilometers!

Geography

Russia’s geography is incredibly diverse, with landscapes ranging from vast tundras and icy waters to lush forests and soaring mountain ranges:

  • The expansive Siberian Taiga and the Ural Mountains
  • The Arctic coastline and the remote Kamchatka Peninsula
  • Lake Baikal, the world’s deepest freshwater lake
  • The Volga River, the longest river in Europe

Culture

Russia’s cultural heritage is rich and unique, shaped by centuries of art, literature, and history:

  • The works of legendary authors like Tolstoy, Dostoevsky, and Pushkin
  • The famous Russian ballet, with renowned institutions like the Bolshoi Theatre
  • Traditional cuisine featuring dishes like borscht, pelmeni, and blini
  • Festivals such as Maslenitsa and Russian Orthodox Christmas

Tourism Highlights

Top places to visit in Russia include:

  • The Red Square and the Kremlin in Moscow
  • The Hermitage Museum in St. Petersburg
  • Lake Baikal and the scenic Trans-Siberian Railway
  • The majestic Catherine Palace near St. Petersburg
  • The Kamchatka Peninsula’s volcanic landscapes and wildlife
